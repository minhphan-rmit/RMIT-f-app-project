Project description

Overview

Topic

Numerous people are concerned about nutritional imbalance and the rise in obesity rates. According to Shattuck (2017), obesity rates did not exceed 15% in any U.S. state in 1985, and by 2016 no state had a rate under 20%. Currently, the average obesity rate is over 33%[1]. It is obvious that correcting dietary imbalances will benefit society. As rates of physical activity have decreased, there is an increasing epidemic of obesity (Skinner et al., 2018, cited in Armstrong, 2019)[2].. IT professionals are not exempt from this rule. In particular, persons who spend several hours sitting in chairs and avoid outdoor activities are more likely to be exposed to obesity or underweight risks.

As a result, our group developed an interest in the topic of nutritional imbalance. BMI is one of the well-known and practical measurements of obesity. By dividing one's weight by the square of height, individuals can quickly determine their present physical state. Our group identified shortcomings in other BMI calculators. This was the issue with only giving out the basic necessities of data, such as the present situation and statistics. Our group believed that it would be more practical if it offered suggestions as to what types of management are required beyond just calculating BMI. Therefore, we made the decision to develop a calculator that does not require users to check their BMI, search for exercises online, or find another calculator to see how many calories they consumed today. Users can calculate their daily caloric intake and receive suggestions depending on their BMI using our project. This allows users to know roughly whether they should reduce their diet, exercise, or increase their diet.

[1]Shattuck, M. 2017, Control diet, weight in diabetes fight: As rates of obesity increase, so too do rates of Type 2 diabetes, Appleton, Wis.

[2]Armstrong, S. & Wong, C. (2019) Physical Activity Interventions Improve Quality of Life of Children With Obesity: Should We Expect Them to Also Decrease Body Mass Index? Journal of adolescent health. [Online] 65 (3), 309–310.

Motivation

After the Covid-19 pandemic and a long period of work-from-home, people started realizing how important it is to be healthy both physically and mentally. Based on that realization, we came up with the idea of a virtual assistant for health care. This project can change the habits of the majority of people about their awareness about their health, and help people have a better and healthy life.

Smartphones have become an indispensable part of our life in the past 20 years, everything now can be accessed easily using smartphones from payment, transportation, socialization,... That is why we came up with a cross-platform mobile virtual assistant that is available on your mobile devices such as smartphones, laptops, tablets,... We believe that the trend of mobile applications will last for many years or even when Augmented reality and Virtual reality become popular in the near future, it will be an opportunity for us to make our virtual assistant more “realistic”.

This project is not just a “school project” or normal group project. This project is the replication of real-life IT related projects. As a result, working in this project brings us many benefits. Firstly, we have a chance to work in a professional environment. We do documents like proposals, reports, meeting minutes, papers,...; we do academic research for our project idea; we have to obey different work ethics from the normal projects. Secondly, we learn how to work effectively in a group, a department or something similar. We learn how to keep in touch with people, teammates and other departments; we learn how to make professional communications;... In conclusion, there are many invisible benefits that we can gain from working in a project like technical skills and knowledge besides professional workflow. This makes a good opportunity for us to prove ourselves to the employers that we can fit in their business.

Landscape

Our project idea is not highly distinguishable from the market. There are many similar products with similar functions with our product. We chose 2 products that already exist to give an overview about similar products:

  • Nike Training Club is a fitness app from Nike, Inc.
  • We Do Pulse is a personal health assistant made by PULSE ECOSYSTEM PTE. LTD.

Both products mentioned above have almost the same features as our apps. But these products are consistently receiving bad reviews because of bad user experience. Common problems of the competitors are:

  • The free plans and programs are completely useless.
  • The programs provided are too general and not personalized enough for every user.
  • The User Interface of the app is not attractive and easy-to-use.
  • Most of the time, the users will feel “lonely” because the assistant they have is not really an assistant.

Understanding the users’ problems, our aim is to solve all the problems mentioned above. Besides providing programs by computer calculation and maybe AI in the far future, we are planning to provide a platform to connect trainers and users. This feature will make our product more user-friendly and more customizable. Furthermore, we are focusing on developing UX and UI together so as to make the app more easy-to-use and attractive.

Detailed description

Aims

Based on the disadvantages of other similar products that we mentioned above, we can easily define our most important aim for this project:

A “real” virtual trainer

Being fit and healthy does not mean that you have to work hard like a real body builder. Usually, they are simple exercises at home, a morning trekking in your nearby park, … But sometimes, you may lose your track, you don’t know where to start or continue your track. This is when a virtual trainer comes in handy. A virtual trainer not only provides you a training program but also tracks your progress, giving you personalized advice based on your real status.

In order to be a “real” virtual trainer, we have defined some goals for our process of development:

The most attractive UI

For us, UI is one of the most important parts of a mobile app's success. An attractive and energetic UI is a big motivation for our users. Looking and using an app that is colorful and motivating gives our users more encouragement to work out and stay healthy.

The best UX

Building a system that has good user experience is a big challenge for every project. There are two main points that we will focus on the UX of our product: simplify the way of using the app by easing the connection between users and trainers; personalize the app as much as possible for every user so that they will feel cared and motivated.

Verified trainers

Our product is not only for users who want to stay healthy but also trainers who are passionate in body building and willing to share their knowledge to help other people stay healthy. This will help develop their passion and bring them a source of income.
